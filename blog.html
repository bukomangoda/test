COVID-19 UPDATE

COVID-19 PROPERTY DETAILS

Responsibility

It is a primary goal for us to provide a safer environment for our visitors and workers. It is everyone's obligation to make this happen. Please help us improve the safety of our public spaces by following local legislation, social distance (keeping at least 6 feet or 2 meters apart from other guests and hotel staff), and wearing facial covers anytime you're in the hotel's public areas. We really appreciate your patience and understanding.

 

Contactless experience

Signage throughout our hotels reminding guests to keep social distance, occupancy limitations and seating capacity reductions in our restaurants in conformity with municipal and state laws, and barriers built at front desks are just a few of the measures we've taken

At all times, associates will be observed wearing face masks and gloves, and all places will be cleansed according to local health regulations. In the public areas, all visitors must wear face masks.

For a seamless arrival experience, online payment choices will be recommended as part of the pre-arrival experience

If they are not already wearing one, every visitors will be given one

All tables will be sterilized, and a minimum distance of 6 feet will be maintained between them.

 

 Follow cleaning protocols

Improved Public Space Cleaning: We've improved the frequency of cleaning and disinfection, especially in high-traffic areas like toilets, elevators, and escalators, and added additional hand sanitizing stations.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE): Staff members will wear PPE depending on the activity they are conducting and the local authorities' directions.

When in public locations, guests must wear personal facial covers.

To assist our existing stringent cleanliness requirements, we're using advanced technology like electrostatic sprayers with hospital-grade disinfectants.

The Hotel Cleanliness Champion will keep track of how often cleaning schedules are followed in all high-touch areas of the hotel
